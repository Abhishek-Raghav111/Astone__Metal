About

My introduction

We started our business operation in the year 2004 with a modest beginning and achieved success with our expertise and in-depth knowledge. Establishing ourselves as a prominent brand, we, ASTONE PRE-ENGINEERED BUILDING & ROOFING SYSTEMS, are today a name to reckon with. We have set up an outstanding example for others showing that rapid growth is possible by strictly maintaining quality of products and serving the clients with complete dedication. We are engaged in the industry as a distinguished manufacturer, trader and distributor dealing in a number of products like Pre Engineered Building Systems, Pre Structured Building Systems, Pre Engineering Steel Buildings, Container/Modular Site Offices, Insulating Roofing and Panel, Trapezoidal Profile and False Ceiling System.
